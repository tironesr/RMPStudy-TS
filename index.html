Sailing to Allyship

Please read the information below before connecting with your Sailing to Allyship partner.


Description

Welcome to the Sailing to Allyship program!

In a few moments you will be connected to a White partner who is interested in allyship using this video conference platform. The goal of the program is to open lines of communication between White and non-White Americans about how to be a good ally.

As a reminder, this program is volunteer only and you will not be compensated for your time.




When you are ready to begin, please press the "Enable Video/Audio" button to set up your camera and microphone.

Your browser will ask for permission to access your camera and then your microphone. Please select "Always Allow". If you would only like to connect with audio, deny access to your camera and accept access to your microphone. You should be able to see your video feed in in the corner of the webpage. If you do not, try refreshing the page and clicking "Enable Video/Audio" again.

When you are set up and ready to join the call with your partner, please click the "Connect" button
If you do not see the "Connect" button, make sure to click "Enable Video/Audio" and set up your camera and microphone first.
